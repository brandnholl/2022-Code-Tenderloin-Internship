Why otters are great

  1. Most sea otters call Alaska home
  2. Sea otters eat 25 percent of their body weight in food every day
  3. Sea otters have the thickest fur of any animal.
  4. Sea otters can have a pup any time of the year.
  5. The otter is one of the few mammals that use tools.
